City Game

A city building tile-style game. The player is able to drag and place tiles. Most tiles have unique effects. The player wins when he collects a certain number of chaos emeralds. Factors like happiness, effect the rate at which these chaos emeralds are found. The player can also zoom in and out and scroll around the city.
